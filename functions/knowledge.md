## Organization

- **Name:** UCSD × CRS (Collegiate Racing Series at UC San Diego)
- **What we are:** A fully structured, student-led motorsport organization —
  engineering, driving, pit crew, media, and ops working as one race team.
- **Website:** https://ucsdxcrs.web.app
- **Instagram:** https://www.instagram.com/crs_ucsd/
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/ucsd-crs

## How joining works (high level)

1. **Join & onboard** — Apply, meet the leads, and land in a role. You get the
   season calendar, shop norms, and a clear first assignment.
2. **Train across roles** — Build nights, sim sessions, and crew drills.
   Cross-train so you understand the car, the stop, and the weekend.
3. **Race & deliver** — Compete in the Collegiate Racing Series with a full
   student-run program behind you.

## Roles / teams

### Driver

- Focus: race craft, consistency, and preparation for Collegiate Racing Series weekends.
- Experience: all levels welcome to ask; prior karting/track experience helps but
  is not required to start a conversation with the team.

### Engineer

- Focus: chassis, powertrain, aero, and systems on the car.
- Good fit for students who want hands-on vehicle engineering and data-driven iteration.

### PIT Crew

- Focus: timed stops — tires, fuel, safety checks, and drilled execution.
- Prior experience is not required if you are ready to practice and show up consistently.

### Media Team

- Focus: race-weekend storytelling, photography/video coverage, and team presence.

### Content Creator

- Focus: ongoing content that shares the team’s work, culture, and race weekends.

### Operation Team

- Focus: logistics, coordination, and keeping race weekends and sponsors moving.
